Trekking Spots

Nandi Hills

Located about 60 kilometers from Bangalore, Nandi Hills offers breathtaking views and moderate trekking trails.

Savandurga

Savandurga is known for its challenging trek routes and scenic beauty, perfect for adventure enthusiasts.

Kumara Parvatha

Kumara Parvatha, also known as Pushpagiri, is a popular trekking destination in the Western Ghats near Coorg, approximately 270 kilometers from Bangalore.

Kudremukh

Kudremukh is famous for its spectacular landscapes, rolling hills, and diverse flora and fauna. It's located around 330 kilometers from Bangalore.

Skandagiri

Skandagiri, also known as Kalavara Durga, is a hill fortress located approximately 70 kilometers from Bangalore, offering a popular night trekking experience.
