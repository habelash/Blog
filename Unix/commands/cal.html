cal Command

The cal command in Unix/Linux displays a calendar of the current month or a specific month/year.

Syntax
cal [options] [month] [year]
Options
  • -3 - Display the previous, current, and next month
  • -y - Display the entire year
Examples
cal
cal 7 2024
cal -3
cal -y
