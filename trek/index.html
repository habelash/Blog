Trekking Spots Near Bangalore and Karnataka

Nandi Hills

Located about 60 kilometers from Bangalore, Nandi Hills offers breathtaking views and moderate trekking trails.

Trek Duration: 2-3 hours

Altitude: Approximately 1,478 meters

Difficulty Level: Easy to moderate

Savandurga

Savandurga is known for its challenging trek routes and scenic beauty, perfect for adventure enthusiasts.

Trek Duration: 4-5 hours

Altitude: Approximately 1,226 meters

Difficulty Level: Moderate to difficult

Skandagiri

Skandagiri, also known as Kalavara Durga, is a hill fortress located approximately 70 kilometers from Bangalore, offering a popular night trekking experience.

Trek Duration: 3-4 hours

Altitude: Approximately 1,450 meters

Difficulty Level: Moderate

Anthargange

Anthargange is known for its unique rock formations and caves, located around 70 kilometers from Bangalore.

Trek Duration: 3-4 hours

Altitude: Approximately 1,712 meters

Difficulty Level: Easy to moderate

Ramanagara

Ramanagara is famous for its rocky hills and was the shooting location for the movie "Sholay". It's about 50 kilometers from Bangalore.

Trek Duration: 2-3 hours

Altitude: Approximately 747 meters

Difficulty Level: Easy

Shivagange

Shivagange is a hill peak with a sacred temple and offers trekking opportunities along with stunning views, located about 60 kilometers from Bangalore.

Trek Duration: 3-4 hours

Altitude: Approximately 804 meters

Difficulty Level: Moderate

Channarayana Durga

Channarayana Durga is a historical fort situated around 100 kilometers from Bangalore, offering a challenging trekking experience.

Trek Duration: 4-5 hours

Altitude: Approximately 1,188 meters

Difficulty Level: Moderate to difficult

Kumara Parvatha

Kumara Parvatha, also known as Pushpagiri, is a popular trekking destination in the Western Ghats near Coorg, approximately 270 kilometers from Bangalore.

Trek Duration: 2 days (overnight trek)

Altitude: Approximately 1,712 meters

Difficulty Level: Difficult

Kodachadri

Kodachadri is a mountain peak in the Western Ghats, known for its lush forests and scenic beauty, located around 400 kilometers from Bangalore.

Trek Duration: 2 days (overnight trek)

Altitude: Approximately 1,343 meters

Difficulty Level: Moderate to difficult

Kudremukh

Kudremukh is famous for its spectacular landscapes, rolling hills, and diverse flora and fauna. It's located around 330 kilometers from Bangalore.

Trek Duration: 2 days (overnight trek)

Altitude: Approximately 1,894 meters

Difficulty Level: Moderate to difficult
